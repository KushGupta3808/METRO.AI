# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## Project: METRO AI

---

## 1. Problem Statement
*   **The Challenge:** International students and expatriates face highly volatile currency exchange corridors. Remittance platforms continuously manipulate retail conversion rates, hiding profit margins behind "zero-fee" marketing campaigns and temporary promotional brackets.
*   **The Impact:** Users lose hundreds of dollars annually due to poor market timing and a lack of centralized, transparent rate aggregation.

---

## 2. Target Audience & Personas
*   **Persona 1: The Budget-Conscious International Student**
    *   *Profile:* Living abroad (e.g., Canada), managing tight personal savings or educational loans, and periodically transferring lump sums home.
    *   *Core Need:* High-precision market-timing to maximize the destination currency yield (e.g., CAD to INR) down to the cent.
*   **Persona 2: The High-Frequency Family Supporter**
    *   *Profile:* Working professional sending fixed portions of their paycheck home on strict monthly schedules.
    *   *Core Need:* Rapid identification of active promotional coupon codes and the lowest structural platform fees available *today*.

---

## 3. Functional Requirements (Scope Matrix)
*   [ ] **Core Aggregator Engine:** Multi-platform rate scraping and processing.
*   [ ] **Interactive Tracking Analytics:** Historical trend mapping and live spread differentials.
*   [ ] **Predictive AI Orchestrator:** Multi-agent news parsing, sentiment weight analysis, and automated "Hold/Send" execution signals.
*   [ ] **User Preference Profiles:** Account registration, tier-tracking, and customized notification webhooks.
